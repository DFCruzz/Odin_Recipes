Chocolate Cake

What it is?

This Chocolate cake recipe is very basic yet very tasty recipe to fill that need to eat a very good cake or a chocolate dessert, or maybe BOTH!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of water
  • Â½ cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of all purpoused flour
  • 1 cup powdered chocolate
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 spoon of baking powder

How to make it:

  1. Mix the flour, powdered chocolate, baking powder and sugar in a big bowl until you get a good mixture, then add the eggs, vegetable oil and water and beat it until you get a very smooth dough
  2. Put the dough in a cake pan and cook it in a low heat oven for about 25 to 30 minutes
