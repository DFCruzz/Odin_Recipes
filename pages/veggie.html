(Blender) Veggie Pie

What it is?

This Veggie Pie is a quick to make, very tasty recipe and usually can be done with leftover vegetables found in everybodies fridge

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Â½ cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of all purpoused flour
  • 1 cup of grated cheese
  • salt and powdered black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 spoon on baking powder
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 1 can of peas (drained)
  • 1 chopped, seedless tomato
  • 1 can of corn (drained)

How to make it:

  1. Put the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, flour, baking powder grated cheese, salt and pepper in a blender and blend until you get a homogenous liquid dough
  2. In a bowl, put the carrot, peas, corn and tomatoes and season it to taste
  3. Put half of the dough in oven pan, then spread all the veggies on top of the bowl (the dough is thick enough for the veggies stay on top of it. Then use the other half of the dough to cover all the veggies. Cook for about 35 minutes in medium heat oven
